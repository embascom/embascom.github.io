Assignment 7: Final Project Proposal!

Project Description

Earlier this quarter, my partner and I moved into a new apartment that is bigger and has an additional bathroom. Because of this, we are in need of some furnishings that luckily can be 3D printed. For my final project, I am going to create the things we need through a combination of my own designs and designs from Thingiverse.

Design

I will be creating the following:

  • 1× bar soap holder
  • 1× liquid dish soap holder
  • 2× q-tip holders
  • 2× cotton round holders
  • 1× toothbrush holder

As a stretch goal, I would also like to create the following:

  • Various hooks for throughout the apartment
  • Various drawers dividers for the kitchen and bathroom
  • Various cat toys for my little babies

Methods

For my project, I will use the following techniques and tools:

  • Rhino - For modeling and editing my designs
  • Fusion 360 - For modeling and editing my designs
  • 3D printing - To actually create my pieces and positives for my mold
  • Silicon Modeling and Plaster Casting - To create the pieces that I do not 3D print

Schedule

  • Week 1: May 13 to May 19 - Look through Thingiverse and select what I want to use from that resource versus what I want to design fom scratch
  • Week 2: May 20 to May 26 - Have all of my Rhino/Fusion360 files complete and have almost all, if not all pieces printed and/or molded
  • Week 3: May 27 to May 31 - Have all pieces ready to present for the final project and submit all necessary documentation
